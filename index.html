Research Highlights

Materials Characterization at Triclinic Labs

As Senior Scientific Investigator and Subject Matter Expert for XRPD, XRF, and SEM-EDS instruments:

  • Plan and execute variable humidity and temperature XRD experiments
  • Analyze and interpret complex crystallographic data for pharmaceutical clients
  • Implement advanced statistical and machine learning methods for materials analysis

Energy & Environmental Research (EERC/UND)

Over 22 years contributed to 33 abstracts, 57 articles, 51 proposals, and 51 final reports:

  • Developed image analysis workflow for characterizing porosity in unconventional reservoirs
  • Applied machine learning for segmentation of BSE SEM images
  • Designed and built custom scientific apparatus including gas pycnometer
  • Led characterization of geological samples for CO2 storage applications
